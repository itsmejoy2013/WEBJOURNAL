OTHER EMERGING TECHNOLOGY

BIOTECHNOLOGY

Biotechnology is the application of biological processes, organisms, or systems to develop new products or improve existing ones.

NANOTECHNOLOGY

Nanotechnology is the study and manipulation of matter on an extremely small scale, typically between 1 and 100 nanometers.

BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGY

Blockchain technology is a distributed ledger technology that allows multiple parties to have a synchronized, tamper-proof and transparent record of transactions and data in a decentralized network.

COMPUTER VISION

Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence that enables machines to interpret and understand the visual world through digital images or videos.
